## A) Global (site‑wide)

### A1) Tone
- Clear, practical, short sentences.
- Lead with the outcome, then explain the process.
- In EN locale “preflight” can be understood by pros, but we still prefer **“File check”** for clarity.

### A2) Terminology (canonical)
- **File check** = automatic check + human review before printing.
- **60 cm sheet** = a single 60 cm wide layout (what some call a “gang sheet”).
- **Free test print** = 1 test print (one design of your choice).
- **Peel right away** = hot peel.
- **Summary** is OK in EN, but UA/RU must use local words.
