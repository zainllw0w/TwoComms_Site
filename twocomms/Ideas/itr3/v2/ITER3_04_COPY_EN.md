## I) FAQ `/faq/`

### SEO (meta)
- **Title:** `DTF FAQ (60 cm) — answers to common questions | TwoComms`
- **Description:** `File formats, timing, delivery, transfer, care. If you didn't find an answer — message us in Telegram.`
- **H1:** `FAQ`

### Questions / answers (format: Short → Details)

1) **`Which formats work?`**  
   **Short:** PNG (transparent background) or PDF.  
   **Details:** Ideally PNG 300 DPI with transparent background in the final size. Large files can be sent via link (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.).

2) **`What does "peel while hot" mean?`**  
   **Short:** The film peels right after pressing.  
   **Details:** Unlike "cold peel", no need to wait for cooling. Saves time on large runs.

3) **`How do I know my file is OK?`**  
   **Short:** Send it — we'll check and tell you.  
   **Details:** We check resolution, transparency, minimum lines and text. If anything needs fixing, we'll tell you exactly what.

4) **`Do you handle the white layer?`**  
   **Short:** Yes, the white layer is on us.  
   **Details:** We tune it for your design and fabric. You don't need to create a white underbase — we handle it.

5) **`Do you ship across Ukraine?`**  
   **Short:** Yes, via Nova Poshta.  
   **Details:** After the file check, we confirm details, print, and ship. You'll receive a tracking number.

6) **`Can it be urgent?`**  
   **Short:** Yes, message us in Telegram with your deadline.  
   **Details:** Tell us the date and volume — we'll say if we can make it. Small urgent runs are usually fine.

7) **`What if my file isn't the right size?`**  
   **Short:** Message us — we'll advise or resize.  
   **Details:** We'll tell you the right size or resize it without losing quality.

8) **`Can a DTF print peel off?`**  
   **Short:** With proper transfer — it holds well.  
   **Details:** The key is temperature, time, and pressure. If unsure — ask us for transfer settings.
