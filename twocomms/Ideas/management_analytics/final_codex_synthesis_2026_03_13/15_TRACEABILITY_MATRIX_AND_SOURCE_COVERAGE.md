## Anti-Loss Summary

Nothing important is intentionally left floating outside the canonical package:
- baseline authoritative themes are absorbed into thematic contracts;
- improvement deltas are integrated into the same contracts;
- code-reality and readiness audits are integrated into code map, governance, testing and blueprint files;
- research prompts and report-style documents remain available as `reference only` and are not allowed to silently become runtime truth.
